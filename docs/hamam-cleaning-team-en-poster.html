Hammam Team – Closing & Cleaning Flow

1. Guest Interaction

  1. Thank the guest and recap the ritual and ingredients used.
  2. If the guest is happy, say “see you soon”; note any concerns and alert the manager.
  3. For defects, capture photo + code (HAM_05) + short note and send via WhatsApp/Slack.

2. Defect & Damage Reporting

  1. Small cracks/spills: cordon the area, document with a photo, inform maintenance promptly.
  2. Pool/jacuzzi alerts: shut the system, log a maintenance request, and prepare an alternate room if needed.
  3. Water/heating regulator fault: call the technician, log it, and offer a backup room to the guest.

3. Detailed Cleaning Rounds

  1. Round 1: Clean the steam room glass, stone benches, textured walls, and rope doors.
  2. Round 2: Dry any liquids, then wipe the floor with certified disinfectant.
  3. Round 3: Sanitize pool edges, showers, towel trays, accessories with antiseptic.
  4. Round 4: Check the air purifier and aroma diffuser; refresh plants (laurel/lavender) with fresh water.
  5. Round 5: Apply “cleaning complete” tag and log notes in the digital form.

4. Resources & Sustainability

  • Record chemicals + amounts to avoid wrong mixtures.
  • Put soiled textiles into red bags before sending to laundry.
  • Ventilate after lights/water are off; keep doors closed until the next guest arrives.
