Role Selection

Lock the role filter to Reception on front desk machines and Therapist on treatment devices; stay within your role’s cards.

TR / EN Rule

One technical surface, one logic. TR staff follow the TR guide, EN staff follow this guide; the prompts remain shared.

Kids Safe Rules

  • Always ask for parental permission.
  • Display and follow the SafeNote—honor quiet, comfort, and consent.
  • No medical promises; stick to wellness/ritual language.

Empty Screen Fix
