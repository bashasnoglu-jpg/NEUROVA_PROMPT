Sports Massage

Lactic acid removal, dynamic stretching. Special therapy supporting sports performance and accelerating recovery.

About the Ritual

Sports massage aims to reduce tension accumulated in areas working intensely in an active lifestyle and give the body a feeling of "recovery". Depending on your needs and sports routine, one of the following variations is applied:

1. Pre-Workout (Activation)

Invigorating sports massage supporting movement patterns and preparing the body for training with rhythmic touches.

Pre-Workout Massage is applied to reduce stiffness accumulated in muscles, fascia, and periarticular tissues before training, preparing the body for natural movement patterns. Medium pressure and rhythmic touches provide openness around the joints.
